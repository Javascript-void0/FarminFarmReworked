## Reference

 > Numeral

Numbers on the server are represented differently than conventional 'K', 'M', 'B', 'T', etc. Instead of these, the server represents thousands, millions, billions, etc. with lowercase alphabet letters. Starting with 'a', representing thousands, then to 'b', representing millions.

# Ex: 12.2a = ~12,200 (rounded)

# Ex: 109.3b = ~109,300,000

# Ex: 4.8c = ~4,800,000,000


Knowing exact numbers isn't really important, but you can often hover over these abbreviated numbers in chat to reveal its exact, un-rounded value. Skript's variables allow the numbers to go up to 'f'.

 > Discord

Linking :


To link your Minecraft account to Discord, first use /link in-game. It will give you a five digit code. Remember the code, or click to copy the whole command to your clipboard. The code is valid for 1 minute.


Then go to discord and type .link [code] in #code, or paste it from your clipboard.


Once linked, you will recieve 10b coins and 10a wrath.

Overlay :


In-game chat (Minecraft chat) is disabled because it might interfere with the chat-ui. But, chat is still avaiable in Discord in #umoria. If you really want to view chat, I would recommend either having Discord open on the side, or using Discord's overlay feature.


To use the overlay, go into settings and add Minecraft to Activity Status. Make sure OVERLAY: ON in Activity Status for Minecraft. Then go to Game Overlay menu and make sure it is also on. Open minecraft and press Shift + ` (or whatever keybind you have it set to)

## Dungeon


Rooms throughout the dungeon are randomized. Location of chests and mob types are also randomized (except special rooms).


Dungeons are split into floors and rooms. Each floor can have anywhere from 10 to 20 rooms. You ascend a floor after going through every room on the floor.

 > Blocks

Lodestone : Generates next room


Trapped Chest : Un-opened chest. Generated randomly throughout rooms. Shift + Left Click to loot all, except for arrows.


Cracked Stone Bricks : You can break cracked stone bricks with a pickaxe.


## Player

 > Stats

Health :


Health is taken once defense is depleted. Health does not regenerate naturally. It costs 3 Upgrade Points to increase max health by 10.


Defense :


Defense stat regenerates overtime, until it reaches its max. Defense is taken before health. It costs 50 Upgrade Points to increase max defense by 1.


Damage :


Damage stat is base damage. It costs 1 Upgrade Point to increase damage by 10.


Level :


Leveling up gives the player 1 Upgrade Point, used in Upgrades menu to increase health, defense, or damage.

 > Equipment

Damage :


Using tools not meant for melee results in less damage.


Green damage indicators from attacking show damage done with damage stat.


Gray damage indicators from attacking show damage done with sharpness or power.

Defense :


All four armor pieces share the same enchantments, so they are all calculated once, not x4.


For each level of Unbreaking, the damage the player takes is reduced by the level of Unbreaking. Defense indicators are placed in brackets '[' and ']'.


For the shield, each level of Protection allows the cooldown of the shield to shortened with this simple formula: cooldown = 1 - (level of protection / 10). The minimum cooldown is 0.3s.
